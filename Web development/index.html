Education

I always have a deep interest in Mathematics, and finally it lead to Bronze medal in International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO), 2008. I got my BSc degress at Mathematics from Midle East Technical University, in Ankara, Turkey. Thereafter I decided to explore the venues where I can apply these mathematical models. I firtst tried Quantitative Finance at ETH Zurich by enrolling into MSc in Applied Mathematics. I didn't find the thrill I was looking for.
